Classic Pancakes


Ingridients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (or oil)

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Mix wet ingredients In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients. Stir gently until just combined. Small lumps are okay—don't overmix.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter or oil.
  5. Pour about ¼ cup batter per pancake onto the pan. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set (about 1 - 2 minutes).
  6. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown (about 1 minute).
  7. Stack them up and serve warm with butter, syrup, fruit, or whatever you love.
