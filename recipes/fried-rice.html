Vegetable Fried-Rice


Ingridients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • ½ cup carrots (chopped)
  • ½ cup green peas
  • ½ cup bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon spring onions (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and let it cool completely. Cold rice works best for fried rice.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and garlic. Sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add carrots, peas, and bell peppers. Cook for 3 - 4 minutes until slightly soft.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix well.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce, then add salt and black pepper.
  7. Stir everything together and cook for 2 - 3 minutes.
  8. Turn off the heat and garnish with spring onions if desired.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
