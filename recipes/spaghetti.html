Spaghetti Aglio e Olio


Origin: Italy (Naples)

A simple pasta dish made with garlic (agilio) and olive oil(olio). It's known for being delicious with minimal ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic (sliced thin)
  • 1/4 cup olive Oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley
  • Parmeson cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water al denate.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic slices, and cook until lightly golden.
  3. Add red pepper flakes.
  4. Drain pasta (save some water) and add to the pan. Toss well.
  5. Add pasta water if it looks dry. Mix in parsley.
  6. Serve with toasted bread or Salad.
